**What the Hell Happened to Wendy’s?**

Right now, as you read this very story, a Wendy’s somewhere is closing.
In February, the
chain [announced](https://apnews.com/article/wendys-value-restaurants-closing-cfd9a7865904ce569044ded5a205cf73) that
it would shutter at least 300 locations in the first half of 2026 in a
ketchup-tinted bloodbath that followed a [shocking 11.3 percent drop in
same-store
sales](https://www.nrn.com/quick-service/wendy-s-takes-sharp-u-turn-back-to-its-core-business)–the
industry’s key measure of year-over-year growth–at the end of last year.
Whatever the final death tally, the losses will add up to somewhere
around 5 to 6 percent of Wendy’s total store count.
